Secret Sauce

  • Time: 5 Minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Sour Cream 1/2 Cup
Hot Sauce 3 Tbsp
Lime Juice 1 Tbsp

Step 1: Put all the ingredients in a mixing bowl.

Step 2: Whip all of the ingredients together using a tiny wisk or a fork.

Step 3: Enjoy on your favorite dish that needs a spicy/tangy kick!

Chocolate Milk

  • Time: 2 Minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1 1/2 Cups
Cocoa Powder 3 Tbsp

Step 1: Pour the milk into a glass big enough for stirring.

Step 2: Whip all of the ingredients together using a tiny wisk or a fork.

Step 3: Enjoy as a sweet treat after a nice meal!

Salad

  • Time: 20 Minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Lettuce 1/2 a head
Tomato 1 whole
Avacados 2 whole
Cheddar Cheese 1 cup shredded
Cilantro 1/4 cup chopped
Olive Oil 1/2 Cup
Lime Juice 3 Tbsp

Step 1: Prepare the vegtables by dicing (tomatoes), slicing (avacados), and chopping them (lettuce & cilantro).

Step 2: Set the cilantro to the side. Place the remaining vegtables into a large mixing bowl.

Step 3: Whisk the cilantro, olive oil, and lime together in a seperate bowl.

Step 4: Combine the cilantro dressing with the vegtable salad and enjoy!
